South Lyon, USA

Location

South Lyon hugs the Southwest corner of Oakland County. It is bordered by Livingston County to the West and Washtenaw County to the South.

About

Weather

South Lyon suffers from the same weather patterns as much of Michigan.

Commerce

Visit quaint and cool stores downtown!

High School Sport

High school sports are taken very seriously here. You will see the Lyon Pride billboards about town.

Things To Do

  • Visit Downtown Stores and Restaurants
  • Ride bikes on the Rail Trail
  • Visit the historic Witch's Hat depot and then have a beer at the brewery!
